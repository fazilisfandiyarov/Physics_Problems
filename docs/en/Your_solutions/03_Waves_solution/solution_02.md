# Harmonic Motion Analysis

This analysis determines the physical properties of a spring-mass system based on its oscillation equation.

## Given Parameters
* **Equation of Motion:** $x(t) = 0.2 \cos(10\pi t)$
* **Mass ($m$):** $10 \text{ kg}$

---

## 1. Spring Constant ($k$)

The angular frequency $\omega$ is extracted from the cosine argument:
$$\omega = 10\pi \text{ rad/s}$$

Using the formula $k = m\omega^2$:
$$k = 10 \times (10\pi)^2 = 1000\pi^2$$
**Result:** $k \approx 9869.6 \text{ N/m}$

---

## 2. Total Mechanical Energy ($E_{total}$)

Total energy in Simple Harmonic Motion is given by:
$$E_{total} = \frac{1}{2} k A^2$$

Where $A = 0.2 \text{ m}$:
$$E_{total} = \frac{1}{2} (1000\pi^2)(0.2)^2$$
$$E_{total} = 20\pi^2$$
**Result:** $E_{total} \approx 197.39 \text{ J}$
