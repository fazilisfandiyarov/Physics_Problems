## 5. Echo Ranging

A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo 1 second later.

Speed of sound in air: 343 m/s

The sound travels to the cliff and back, so the total distance is:

d = v × t  
d = 343 × 1 = 343 m

Distance to the cliff is half of this:

d = 343 / 2 = 171.5 m

### Final Answer:
**171.5 meters**
