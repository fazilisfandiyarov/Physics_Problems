# ⚡ Electron vs Proton Force Comparison

This project compares **electric force** and **gravitational force** between an electron and a proton in a hydrogen atom.

---

## 📌 Theory

### Electric Force (Coulomb Law)
F_e = k e² / r²

### Gravitational Force
F_g = G m_e m_p / r²

### Key Insight
Both forces depend on 1/r², so distance cancels in the ratio:

F_e / F_g = (k e²) / (G m_e m_p)

---

## 🚀 Result

- Electric force is ~10³⁹ times stronger than gravity at atomic scale.
